About This Project

People who write down their goals are more likely to achieve them; I was curious how far I could go by coding them.

Learning shouldn't stop after graduation, and traveling hones appreciation for the joys of being home. We are given little time to explore a vast expanse of earth and emotion; let's not waste it.

This is not a comprehensive list, and I happily take suggestions at LMacGuidwin at Gmail.

About Me

I do user experience at Razorfish in a tiny town called Manhattan. This is my final project for the front-end web development course I took at General Assembly. See more of my work on my site.
